Forward
     Keyboard/Mouse: W
     Controller: Left Analog U

Backward
     Keyboard/Mouse: S
     Controller: Left Analog D

Strafe Left/Right
     Keyboard/Mouse: A/D
     Controller: Left Analog L/R

Rotate Left/Right (Yaw)
     Keyboard/Mouse: Mouse Horizontal
     Controller: Right Analog L/R

Pitch Up/Down (Pitch)
     Keyboard/Mouse: Mouse Vertical
     Controller: Right Analog U/D

Roll Left/Right (Roll)
     Keyboard/Mouse: Q/E
     Controller: Bumper L/R
